About Me

Data is my language.
Insights are my output.

I am a detail-oriented data analyst with a strong foundation in transforming raw, messy datasets into structured, meaningful information that drives real business decisions. My work sits at the intersection of analytical rigour and clear communication.

Proficient in Excel, Power BI, and SQL, I handle every stage of the data lifecycle — from cleaning and structuring untidy data to building interactive dashboards and writing reports that stakeholders can actually act on.

I approach every dataset with a problem-solving mindset, asking the right questions before diving into the numbers. I believe that the best analysis is not the most complex — it's the most clearly communicated. I am committed to continuous learning and delivering results that genuinely move the needle.
